Workout Routines for Beginners

These routines are simple, great for people starting out, consistency, and are low-barrier to entry.

Full Body Workout

  • Plank - 30 seconds: Strengthens the core and helps grow stability
  • Push-ups - 3x8: Builds chest, shoulders, and core strength by using your whole body for the exercise.
  • Bodyweight Squats - 3x12: Great for building leg strength and overall stablity without the use of equipment.
  • Dumbbell Rows - 3x10: Helps balance posture by working your back muscles.

Beginner Cardio Options

Cardio is important for overall health and can be done in many ways. Here are some beginner-friendly options:

  • Brisk Walking - 10-20 minutes:It's the least taxing way to increase your own heart rate.
  • Jump Rope - minute on/1 minute off for 5 rounds: Full-body cardio that helps with coordination.
  • Stationary Bike - 10-15 minutes:Cardio that isn't as challenging for begginers and is easier on the knees than the treadmill.

Upper Body Workout

Focuses on building begginer strength in you chest, shoulders, and your arms.

  • Chest Press - 3x8:A easy way to begin builing your chest and triceps.
  • Tricep Dips - 3x10:Strengthens the back your arms and gets it used to pushing.
  • Bicep Curls - 3x10: The most simple way to beggin lifting weights as well as doing it saftely.
